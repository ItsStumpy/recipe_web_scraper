

John’s Grandma’s Pie Crust
--------------------------







 [Print Recipe](https://recipes.dotlag.info/wprm_print/52)


 



 


 
Servings 1 double-crust pie


### Ingredients

#### Top Crust

* 1 1/2 cups flour
* 2 tsp sugar
* 1 pinch salt
* 1/2 cup neutral oil
* 3 tbs milk
#### Bottom Crust

* 2 1.2 cups flour
* 1 tbsp sugar
* 1 pinch salt
* 3/4 cup neutral oil
* 4 tbs milk

### Instructions

* In two separate bowls whisk together dry ingredients, and then add in wet ingredients and mix.
